Curiosity
  • Launch date: 26 November 2011; Landed: 6 August 2012
  • Mission: determine whether the Red Planet ever was habitable to microbial life.
  • Location: Gale Crater
  • Curiosity found sulfur, nitrogen, oxygen, phosphorus and carbon-- key ingredients necessary for life.
    • Spirit
  • Mission: search for a range of rocks and soil types and then look for clues for past water activity on Mars.
  • Launch date:June 10,2003; Landed: January 4,2004
  • Location: Gusev Crater
  • Spirit found evidence that Mars was once much wetter than it is today and helped scientists better understand the Martian wind.
    • Opportunity
  • Launch date:July 8, 2003;Landed:January 25, 2004
  • Mission: to find more clues about the history of water.
